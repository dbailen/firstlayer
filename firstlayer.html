Text & Dimension Styles

Configure text styles and dimension styles in the Styles & Dims tab. Each dim style has its own name, arrow type, and optional variable overrides — blank fields auto-calculate from your scale and units. Multiple dim styles are supported (e.g. STD-DIM and DETAIL-DIM).

Saving & Sharing

Use ⊞ Presets to save your entire configuration — layers, text styles, dim styles, palette, and all settings — as a named preset in your browser. Export presets as JSON to share your standard with a teammate or back it up. They import the JSON, load the preset, and generate the same LISP or SCR you do.
